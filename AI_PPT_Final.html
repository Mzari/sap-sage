⏰ The Journey of AI

1950

💭 The Dream Begins

Alan Turing asks: "Can machines think?" First AI programs play checkers.

1950-97

❄️ The AI Winter

Limited computing power, no internet data, and high costs kept AI in research labs for 40+ years.

1997

♟️ AI Beats World Champion

IBM's Deep Blue defeats chess champion Garry Kasparov

2000s

🚀 The Breakthrough

Internet provides massive data, computers get powerful—Siri, Netflix, spam filters emerge.

2010-20

📈 AI Everywhere

Face recognition, self-driving cars, Alexa—AI becomes part of daily life.

2025

🎯 The AI Revolution NOW

ChatGPT hits 100M users in 2 months! AI creates images, writes code, makes videos—AI Agents work independently.
