⏰ The Journey of AI

1950

💭 The Dream Begins

Alan Turing asks: "Can machines think?" - First AI programs created, computers could play checkers

1950-1997

❄️ The AI Winter - Why Progress Was Slow

Computers too weak: Not enough processing power • No data: No internet to learn from • Too expensive: Only big labs could afford it • Result: AI stayed in research labs for 40+ years

2000s

🚀 The Breakthrough

Internet provides massive data, computers get powerful, AI enters real products - Siri, Netflix recommendations, spam filters

2010-2020

📈 AI Everywhere

Face recognition, self-driving cars, Alexa, voice assistants become common in daily life

2025

🎯 The AI Revolution NOW

ChatGPT (Nov 2022) - 100M users in 2 months! AI creates images, writes code, makes videos, and now AI Agents can complete entire jobs independently
